Hello! My name is Jeff. Welcome to my learning journal.

I joined the Scrimba Bootcamp in May 2023 after serendipitously discovering Scrimba while browsing through Twitter (now X) for React learning resources. I was so impressed with the quality of the free React Basics course, I decided to join the Bootcamp.

It was an opportunity to get up to speed on the latest HTML, CSS, and JavaScript through building projects, as well as get expert code reviews, level up with weekly workshops, and connect with like-minded people.

My learning journal is an opportunity reflect on challenges faced, my problem-solving process, and hopefully provide learning insights for fellow aspiring developers.
